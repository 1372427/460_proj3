Overview

This page displays US K-12 Education Statistics for the years 1993 to 2015.

Data is from a public database.

How to use this page

Scroll up to see different visualizations. Don't forget to read the explanation for each graph!

To view the data for different years, drag the slider to the desired year or click the "Play" button to see an animation of all years.

For more information about a specific state, please click on the desired state to show.
